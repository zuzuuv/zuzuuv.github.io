Comfortaa

Comfortaa was designed by Johan Askerlund. It is a sans serif rounded and geometric typeface intended to be used in larger sizes. I chose the letterform because of its rounded and geometric features. The typeface reminds me of something that could be used to brand a mattress because of its modern and cloudlike aesthetic. Comfortaa shares similarities with the font Quicksand because of its rounded endings but differs in the endings of certain letters making (Comfortaa) more organic.
